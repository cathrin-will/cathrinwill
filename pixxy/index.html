Pixxy Rules

The aim of the game is to make a picture by clicking the squares in the correct places. The hints are the numbers inline on the right or below.

If the number says 2 it means there are 2 squares somewhere in that row thta are coloured in.

If it says for 2.1 for instance, then that means two are coloured in and then there is a space varying in the number of squares before another coloured in square.

The Levels get harder as you go down the menu, so good luck.

Once all the correct sqaures are coloured in it will make a picture related to the name and you'll have Won!

A little Hint

On the bigger games it can be useful to try complete the large rows first.
